How to Earn Free Solana in 2025
March 2025  ·  BountyWall

Solana has become one of the most popular blockchains for everyday users thanks to its low fees and fast transactions. But did you know you can earn real SOL completely for free? Here's how.

What is an Offerwall?

An offerwall is a platform that rewards users with cryptocurrency for completing simple tasks — things like downloading an app, signing up for a free trial, watching a video, or filling out a survey. Advertisers pay publishers like BountyWall to send them engaged users, and we pass those earnings directly to you in SOL.

How BountyWall Works

BountyWall connects you with Torox, one of the largest offerwall networks in the world. Here's the process:

  1. Sign in with your Google account on the Earn page
  2. Browse available offers and pick ones that interest you
  3. Complete the offer — this usually takes 2 to 10 minutes
  4. Your balance updates automatically
  5. Withdraw your SOL to any Solana wallet address

Tips for Maximizing Earnings

Start with survey offers

Survey offers are the quickest way to earn small amounts consistently. They take 3–5 minutes each and don't require downloading anything.

App install offers pay more

Installing and trying a mobile app typically pays 5–10x more than a survey. If you have a spare device or don't mind cleaning up apps afterwards, these are worth doing.

Check back daily

New offers are added every day. The highest-paying offers fill up quickly, so checking in daily gives you first access.

Is it Really Free?

Yes — you never pay anything. The SOL you earn comes from advertiser budgets, not from other users. Think of it as getting paid for your attention instead of giving it away for free.

The average user earns between $2 and $8 worth of SOL per hour depending on which offers they choose and their location.
